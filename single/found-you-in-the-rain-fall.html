About This Track

"Found You In The Rain Fall" is a testament to the power of human connection in the darkest moments. The imagery of rain and falling suggests loss, uncertainty, and emotional weight—yet within that chaos, there's a discovery. A person, a moment, or a feeling that makes everything worth it.

The production blends melancholic strings with driving beats, creating a soundscape that feels both desperate and hopeful. It's about finding light in darkness, and the realization that sometimes the most beautiful moments come when we least expect them.
