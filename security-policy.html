Security Policy

Vulnerability Disclosure & Safe Harbor

Authorization (Safe Harbor)

If you make a good faith effort to comply with this policy during your security research, we will consider your research to be authorized. We will work with you to understand and resolve the issue quickly, and doubleuofficial.online will not recommend or pursue legal action related to your research.

Research Guidelines

  • / Notify us immediately upon discovery.
  • / Allow reasonable time for remediation.
  • / Avoid privacy violations or data destruction.

Prohibited Actions

  • x Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS) attacks.
  • x Social engineering or phishing.
  • x Accessing or modifying non-owned data.

Reporting a Vulnerability

Please submit detailed reports including reproduction steps and potential impact to:

REACH@DOUBLEUOFFICIAL.ONLINE
Acknowledgement of receipt typically occurs within 3-5 business days. Thank you for helping keep the DoubleU community secure.
